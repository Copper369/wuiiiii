About Me

I am Ayush Karnewar, a third-year Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning) student at DY Patil College of Engineering and Technology, Kolhapur. My academic journey has allowed me to contribute to several research papers, including “ABO Blood Groups and Immunology: A Comprehensive Review,” “The Gravity Hoax: Inertia Mimicking Gravity,” and “Persona.exe: An AI with Consciousness,” all of which have been reviewed by highly qualified professors and reputed universities.

Alongside my research, I have hands-on experience with technologies such as HTML, CSS, Python, and JavaScript with learning PHP and MySQL and APIs. As a DKMS student coordinator, I help organize stem cell donor camps, supporting the cause of fighting blood cancer and other life-threatening diseases.

I am deeply interested in cosmology and enjoy exploring the mysteries of the universe. I also participate in quizzes and debates, which have helped me strengthen my problem-solving and communication skills. I value research, creativity, and continuous learning, and always look forward to new opportunities to collaborate and grow.
